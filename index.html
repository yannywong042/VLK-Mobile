Generative Public Website

Van Lanschot Kempen is dedicated to delivering excellence across private banking, asset management, and investment banking. In response to increasingly diverse market segments, we are actively optimizing our growth models through cutting-edge, Fintech-driven user acquisition strategies.

I designed and implemented a 3-stage acquisition website architecture for Van Lanschot Kempen to enhance user conversion and brand recognition. Based on this, I pioneered a Generative UI framework integrated with Claude Code, enabled a hyper-personalized, intent-driven interface that significantly reduced cognitive load and modernized the traditional private banking experience.
